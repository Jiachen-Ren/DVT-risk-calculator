# DVT Risk Calculator

This web-based calculator estimates the individualized risk of new-onset lower-extremity deep vein thrombosis within 7 days after the first 24 hours of mechanical ventilation in ICU patients.

The calculator is a research prototype for clinical decision support only and should not replace clinical judgment, guideline-based thromboprophylaxis, or lower-extremity ultrasonography.
